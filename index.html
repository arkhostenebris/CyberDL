About Us


Cyber Deadlink (CDL) is an online software agency which provide variety of different computer services. It includes all the online services regarding software development and design. The agency is basically a team of professional freelance developers and designers, and you can hire them according to your needs. It was founded in June 2022, by an experienced cyber security enthusiast, Arkhos Tenebris, who is currently the CEO of the company.
